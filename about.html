Frequently Asked Questions
How do I start investing?
Simply create an account, deposit funds, choose a plan, and invest. The system handles profit calculations automatically.
When can I withdraw?
You can withdraw once your funds are available in your withdrawable balance. Minimum withdrawal is 2,000 UGX.
What is locked balance?
Locked balance represents funds currently invested in active plans. It becomes available after the plan matures.
Is the referral bonus unlimited?
Yes, you can refer unlimited users, but each referral gives only one bonus (10% of their first deposit).
